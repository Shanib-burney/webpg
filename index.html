About

Imperial donuts is an independently owned and family run business that specializes in the creation of artisan donuts. Established in early 2014, we have been blown away by the unexpected sucess and popularity of our carefully crafted donuts. Our donuts are freshly prepared each day prior to sun-rise and amoung our most popular donuts include the honey-gazed donut.

Currently we make 117 different types of donuts on a daily-bases and have two stores in the Toronto area, one store in Montreal and one store in Chicago. Definitely pop by one of our locations to try at least one our donuts but if you can't make it - don't worry! We've recently introduced both a catering service for those special occasions such as weddings & birthday'a and have also recently introduced a delivery service for our 'Special Donut Collection' box which contains 6 different donut flavours.
